A — Bottom sheet

Slides up over the calendar. Quick fields only — title, date/time, who. Drag handle to dismiss. Feels native iOS/Android. For advanced details, a "More options" link opens full form.

B — Full screen form

Takes over the whole screen, Cancel/Save in the top bar. More like the native iOS Calendar "new event" screen. Every field visible at once — nothing hidden behind "More".

C — AI quick-add (type naturally)

Type in plain Danish: "Tandlæge fredag kl 14 med Lars." The assistant (already built!) parses it, shows a preview, you confirm. Fastest possible entry. "Ret" opens full form if needed.

These aren't mutually exclusive — C could be the default with A as fallback for complex events.
